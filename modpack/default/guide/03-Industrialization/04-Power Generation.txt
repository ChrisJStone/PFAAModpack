§2§lPower Generation

One thing that trips up new players often is Railcraft power generation.  It's very easy to blow up IE MV Connectors.

To hook up a Railcraft turbine, use an IC2 MV-Trans between the turbine itself and the MV connector, in step-down mode.  See below!

§1§LNote: The MV-Trans can be anywhere as long as the five-dot side is facing, and touching, any face of the turbine.
